Bhartiya City - Nikoo Homes

Bhartiya City Nikoo Homes 5 is a luxurious apartment project situated on Thanisandra Main Road in Bangalore. The property offers 1 to 4-bedroom apartments, ranging in size from 400 to 2500 square feet of saleable area, and a few studio homes. This upscale real estate project is highly sought-after in North Bengaluru, following the success of Nikoo 4. The 3-bedroom apartments have a built-up area of 1574 and 1686 square feet with carpet area included. The project is available at an attractive sale price and offers a flexible payment plan linked to the construction progress.

Nikoo 5 is a part of the Bhartiya City Mixed-Use Development Township, spanning over 100 acres of valuable real estate. The township comprises a shopping mall, Leela residences and hotels, a school, offices, and social infrastructure. Fill out and submit an Expression of Interest Form (EOI) to reserve a premium unit. This ongoing apartment project is currently under construction and is expected to be completed by 2028, as per RERA regulations.

The address is close to several major landmarks including Manyata Tech Park, Nagavara Outer Ring Road (ORR), Metro Station, International Airport Road, Hebbal, and Hennur Road. Bhartiya City Nikoo Homes 5 offers branded apartments, making it an attractive investment option for those seeking high returns and appreciation. We can provide you with information about the Nikoo 5 launch, including availability of flats, sale prices, master layout and floor plan designs, and payment plans. We also offer a virtual site visit tour and can schedule an online meeting presentation of the project through Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, or Skype

About Us

In 1987, The Bhartiya Group embarked on a journey driven by the belief that good design brings happiness. Originating with leather fashion for names like Hugo Boss and Armani, the journey expanded with founder Snehdeep Aggarwal's global inspirations, witnessing livable cities where cycling and affordable housing thrived.

These insights sparked Bhartiya's mission: to design for happiness and sustainability, transcending aesthetics. Today, their approach encompasses more than fashion it's about enriching lives, fostering sustainable communities, and amplifying well-being. This visionary evolution aligns Bhartiya's ethos with a holistic commitment to enhancing lives worldwide.
